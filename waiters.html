    • Waiter/Waitress Job Description

    We are seeking friendly and attentive waiters/waitresses to join our team at K Gardens. As a waiter/waitress, you will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service, taking orders, and serving food and beverages to our guests.

    Responsibilities:

    • Take orders and serve food and beverages to guests
    • Provide exceptional customer service and ensure guest satisfaction
    • Manage tables, take payments, and handle cash and credit transactions
    • Maintain a clean and organized workspace, including the dining area and service stations
    • Work collaboratively with other staff members to ensure efficient service

    Specifications:

    • High school diploma or equivalent required
    • Previous experience as a waiter/waitress preferred
    • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
    • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and multitask
    • Availability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends
