    Restaurant Supervisor Job Description

    We are seeking an experienced and skilled Restaurant Supervisor to join our team at K Gardens. As a Restaurant Supervisor, you will be responsible for overseeing the daily operations of our restaurant, ensuring exceptional customer service, and supporting the Restaurant Manager.

    Key Responsibilities:

    • Supervise restaurant staff, including servers, bartenders, and hosts/hostesses
    • Ensure exceptional customer service and respond to customer complaints and concerns
    • Assist with inventory management, including ordering supplies and managing stock levels
    • Support the Restaurant Manager with daily operations, including scheduling and staff management
    • Maintain high standards of food quality, safety, and presentation
    • Control labor and inventory costs to maximize profitability

    Specifications:

    • High school diploma or equivalent required
    • Minimum 2 years of experience in a restaurant or food service environment
    • Previous experience as a supervisor or team leader preferred
    • Excellent communication, leadership, and interpersonal skills
    • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and multitask
    • Knowledge of food safety and sanitation regulations
