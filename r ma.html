    • Restaurant Manager Job Description

    We are seeking an experienced and skilled Restaurant Manager to join our team at K Gardens. As a Restaurant Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing the daily operations of our restaurant, ensuring exceptional customer service, and driving sales growth.

    Key Responsibilities:

    • Oversee daily restaurant operations, including food and beverage service, inventory management, and staff supervision
    • Develop and implement strategies to increase sales and revenue growth
    • Ensure exceptional customer service and respond to customer complaints and concerns
    • Manage restaurant staff, including recruitment, training, and performance evaluations
    • Maintain high standards of food quality, safety, and presentation
    • Control labor and inventory costs to maximize to maximize profitability
    • Develop and maintain relationships with suppliers and vendors

    Specifications:

    • Bachelor's degree in Hospitality Management, Business Administration, or related field
    • Minimum 3 years of experience as a Restaurant Manager or Assistant Manager
    • Proven track record of increasing sales and revenue growth
    • Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills
    • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and multitask
    • Knowledge of food safety and sanitation regulations
